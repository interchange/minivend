Upsell

The Upsell page is used to exercise the Upsell marketing feature. The Upsell feature allows you to display an item of better quality and/or price then the one the customer has chosen and give the customer the choice of upgrading to this item. For example, you may choose to display a deluxe toolbox to a customer that is purchasing a standard toolbox.

Functions

OK
After choosing the item you want to upsell to from the upsell_to list, click OK to export the upsell connection.

Cancel
To abandon the upsell task you started without saving the change.

Auto-export
Click this checkbox to enable the auto-export feature. The auto-export feature exports the changes you are committing to the tables described on these pages to the original flat text file. This feature allows you to keep your original files up-to-date and portable. However, while this feature may not hinder the system's performance when exporting a small table, exporting a large table may slow your system.
