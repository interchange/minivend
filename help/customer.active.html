Customers


The customer feature allows you to view a listing of active site customers, inactive site customers, delete customers, deactivate customer, or view detailed customer information. To view detailed customer information click on a customer name(s) and then click view customer. You will be able to view information such as the customers phone, company, email address, billing address, and payment types. To search for a specific customer type the customers name in the text box field and click start list here
