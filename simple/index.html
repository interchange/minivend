Welcome to The Art Store

(The MiniVend Demonstration System)

This is the simple demo for MiniVend 1.03, the freely-redistributable electronic catalog. You can do anything you would with a live catalog -- submit orders, search, etc. If you want to use frames or SSL, you should change to the full-featured demo. This demo only demonstrates the basic features of MiniVend -- there is much more. Reports of anomalous operation will be appreciated.

We have reproductions of some the great are of all ages. Browse the category you like -- or try searching for your favorite artist.

Either way, just press the order button for an item you like! Our electronic order form will automatically add it to your list. Fill out the attached order form to place your order. That's all there is to it!
